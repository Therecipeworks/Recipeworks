Flourless Chocolate Torte

Rich and decadent, perfect for any occasion.

Details

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 50g ground almonds
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water.
  3. Whisk the eggs and sugar until pale and fluffy.
  4. Fold in the melted chocolate mixture.
  5. Gently stir in the ground almonds and salt.
  6. Pour into a greased baking tin and bake for 25-30 minutes.
